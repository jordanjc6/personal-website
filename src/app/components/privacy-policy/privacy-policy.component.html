Castro Cookbook Privacy Policy.

Castro Cookbook does not collect, store, or share any personal information about you.

All text input entered in Castro Cookbook are stored only on your device.
Any photo selected for a recipe through the edit recipe page is only stored on your device.
Upon removing text or photo input in the app it will be completely removed from the app.
Deleting Castro Cookbook deletes all text and photo input in created recipes.
